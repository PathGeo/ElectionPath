Donation Map

Donation Map is a collaboration between inewsource and PathGeo

The map illustrates only contributions from individuals. It excludes donations from such entities as corporations, unions, political committees, as well as individuals located outside the San Diego metro area. It combines contributions to candidates and to the independent committees that support them.

The colorful rings show the percentages of individual donation amounts for each candidate in every Zip Code area. The larger the ring, the bigger the donation amount. Move your mouse on a ring and a popup window will indicate the donation amount for each candidate in that Zip Code.

The gray background shading shows socioeconomic and demographic data from the U.S. Census Bureau (2007-2011 American Community Survey). You can select different layers, such as average family size, population density, or the percentage of age 65 and above, and the background will change.
